Potato Grow Bag

5 gardening mistakes and how to avoid them

1. Soil preparation and nutrients - Plants need water, sunlight and nutrition to grow. Make sure you are providing all these to your vegetable plants. This advice will let you grow plants to their full potential.
2. Nutrients and deficiencies – Always give your plants high quality nutrients and fertilizers. If your growing peppers or eggplants, make sure you give them calcium as well!
3. Be careful of root rot – Use fabric bags or pots with holes to prevent water from building up and drowning roots.
4. Sunlight – Make sure your plants are getting enough sun. You should google your plant before growing it. Ensure you are in the right time to grow the plant based on your zone so it can get adequate sunlight and grow healthy!
5. Not buying seeds from a trusted source – old and brittle seeds are not a good way to grow plants. Make sure you get seeds from someone who has a good reputation. 1.
